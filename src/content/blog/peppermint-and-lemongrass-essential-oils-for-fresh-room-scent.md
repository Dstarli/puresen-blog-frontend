<div class="ps-byline"><span class="ps-brand">Puresen</span><span class="ps-cat">Fresh Scent Guide</span></div>

<p>Peppermint and lemongrass essential oils both work well when you want a room to smell fresh, but they create different moods. Peppermint feels cool and crisp, while lemongrass feels bright, citrus-herbal, and sunny.</p>

<h2>When to Choose Peppermint Essential Oil</h2>

<p>Choose <a href="https://puresen.com/product/peppermint-essential-oil-15ml/">Peppermint Bliss Essential Oil - 15 ml</a> when you want a cool mint scent for a desk, car diffuser, entryway, or morning routine. Peppermint can feel strong, so start with a small number of drops and adjust slowly.</p>

<h2>When to Choose Lemongrass Essential Oil</h2>

<p>Choose <a href="https://puresen.com/product/lemongrass-essential-oil-15ml/">Lemongrass Glow Essential Oil - 15 ml</a> when you want a bright citrus-herbal scent for kitchens, living rooms, or cleaning-day atmosphere. It feels fresh without smelling like a heavy perfume.</p>

<h2>How to Use Fresh Essential Oils Without Overdoing It</h2>

<p>Fresh scents are best when they feel clean, not overpowering. Use your diffuser instructions as the main guide, start small, and give the scent time to spread before adding more.</p>

<h3>Small room tip</h3>

<p>Use less oil in bathrooms, bedrooms, and small offices. Strong scents can become too noticeable in small spaces.</p>

<h3>Large room tip</h3>

<p>For open living areas, allow more time for the scent to spread. More drops are not always better if the airflow is limited.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<p><strong>Q: Which scent is better for a kitchen?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> Lemongrass is usually the easier first choice for kitchens because it has a bright citrus-herbal profile. Peppermint is better when you want a cooler mint scent.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Can I mix peppermint and lemongrass?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> You can blend scents if your diffuser allows it, but start with very small amounts. Peppermint can dominate a blend quickly.</p>

<p><strong>Q: Can I ingest these oils?</strong></p>
<p><strong>A:</strong> No. Puresen essential oils are for aromatic use only and should not be eaten, drunk, or ingested.</p>
